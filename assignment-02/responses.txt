Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.
It travels through electricity, light, or even radio waves. Old Dial-Up, Fiber-
Optics, and WiFi.
What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
Every computer has a unique IP address. A DNS gives those IP addresses readable
names for human eyes. It's hard to remember an IP address when you want to visit
a site.
What is a cookie?
An ID number for your browser and the website that helps your browser remember
things like saved passwords, often-visited websites, clicked links, etc.
